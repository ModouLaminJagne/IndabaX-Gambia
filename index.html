About

IndabaX Gambia 2026

IndabaX Gambia is a local chapter of the Deep Learning Indaba, bringing together professionals, researchers, and AI enthusiasts to connect, share ideas, and explore the latest advancements in Artificial Intelligence.

This two-day event offers attendees an immersive experience with inspiring keynote talks, interactive workshops, and an intense hackathon.

For the 2026 edition, IndabaX Gambia aims to bring the local and regional community together, providing valuable opportunities to grow your network and enhance your expertise in Artificial Intelligence.
